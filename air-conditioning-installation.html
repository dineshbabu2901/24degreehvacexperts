Air Conditioning Installation

Whether it's a single split unit for a bedroom or a fully centralized system for an office tower, correct installation is what determines how well an AC performs and how long it lasts. Our team handles the complete installation process - from site assessment and load calculation to piping, electrical work and commissioning.

What This Service Includes
Split, window, ductable and VRF/VRV AC installation
Cooling load calculation before equipment selection
Copper piping, drainage and electrical wiring
Indoor/outdoor unit positioning for optimal airflow
Post-installation testing and performance check

Why It Matters

Installed Right, The First Time

A poorly installed AC unit wastes energy and fails early. We size and install every system according to the actual heat load of the space, not guesswork - so you get consistent cooling and a longer equipment lifespan.
